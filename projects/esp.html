The Heritage of Kolkata Esplanade

  • Tech Stack: College Project & Event
  • YouTube Link: Esplanade
  • Cultural Kaleidoscope: The Heritage of Kolkata Esplanade is a documentary project developed to capture and showcase the vibrant culture and rich heritage of one of Kolkata's most iconic areas—Esplanade. This project delves into the historical significance, architectural marvels, and cultural traditions that define the heart of Kolkata.
  • Through a blend of compelling visuals, interviews, and historical narratives, the documentary offers an immersive exploration of Esplanade’s evolution from a bustling commercial hub to a center of cultural and historical importance. It highlights the area's influence on local art, architecture, and community life, providing a nuanced perspective on how past and present converge in this dynamic locale.
  • The documentary aims to preserve and celebrate the unique heritage of Kolkata Esplanade, fostering a deeper appreciation of its historical legacy and contemporary significance.

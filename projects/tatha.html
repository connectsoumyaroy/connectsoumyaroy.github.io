Conducted and edited an exclusive interview with Tathagata Mukherjee, one of the most promising Bengali film directors of the current era, for LaughaLaughi. In this heartfelt conversation, he shared his unique perspective on creativity and parenting. His statement, "Every child is the HERO of their parents' script," became a memorable highlight, resonating deeply with readers.

This project emphasized meaningful storytelling and insightful dialogue, showcasing the power of human connection and expression through interviews, as reflected in Tathagata Mukherjee's artistic vision.

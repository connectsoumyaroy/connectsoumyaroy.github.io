AMWELL: Digital Healthcare for the New Normal

  • Tech Stack: College Project & Event
  • YouTube Link: AMWELL
  • During my PG days, I contributed to the development of AMWELL, a comprehensive digital healthcare solution tailored for the post-COVID era. Recognizing the challenges posed by the pandemic, our team created a platform that allows users to connect with doctors virtually, book appointments, search for nearby healthcare providers, and get medicines and COVID-19 safety kits delivered to their doorsteps.
  • The project aimed to make healthcare more accessible by embracing telehealth as the default mode of care. AMWELL not only responded to the immediate needs of the pandemic but also laid the groundwork for a sustainable, digital-first approach to primary care. This experience underscored the importance of innovation in healthcare and demonstrated how technology can be leveraged to meet emerging challenges.

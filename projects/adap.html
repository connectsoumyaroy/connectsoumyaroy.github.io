Resilience in Action: Adapting to Environmental and Social Challenges

  • Tech Stack: College Project & Event
  • YouTube Link: Adapting to Environmental and Social Challenges
  • This project focuses on understanding and addressing the multifaceted impacts of recent environmental and social crises, including the effects of Cyclone Amphan and the ongoing challenges posed by COVID-19. It aims to identify and promote long-lasting values such as Togetherness, Equality, Charity, Eco-Friendliness, and Community-Building.
  • The project will explore strategies for resilience and adaptation, emphasizing the need for proactive measures in the face of such challenges. By leveraging insights from these crises, the project seeks to inspire and drive positive change in both local and global contexts.

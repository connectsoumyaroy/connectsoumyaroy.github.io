Muskurayega India, A Tribute to Resilience

  • Tech Stack: College Project & Event
  • YouTube Link: Muskurayega India
  • Muskurayega India is a heartfelt, homegrown initiative created by the GBS 19-21 Batch to honor our nation's resilience and the courageous individuals fighting against COVID-19. This self-produced video project symbolizes hope and unity, showcasing how we can emerge stronger together despite the challenges posed by the pandemic.
  • The project features a moving portrayal of deserted roads and the joyful echoes of children’s laughter, capturing the essence of perseverance and community spirit. By paying tribute to those on the front lines and encouraging everyone to stay safe and at home, Muskurayega India aims to uplift spirits and reinforce our collective strength in overcoming adversity.
